👤 Profile

I am a Cybersecurity Engineer and CISSP Candidate passionate about combining Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity to build more intelligent, transparent, and resilient systems. I hold a Master’s Degree in Cybersecurity and I’m currently completing a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence, exploring how AI can enhance security automation, detection, and defense strategies.

At Swisscom, I specialize in Secrets Management (HashiCorp Vault, Kubernetes, Terraform) and Security Transparency — an organizational initiative focused on providing clear, data-driven visibility into the company’s overall security posture. This includes developing and maintaining metrics across multiple domains such as risk management, security training, asset and service inventory, maturity models, and vulnerability management. I design internal dashboards and automation pipelines with Python and Streamlit that consolidate data from different systems, enabling leadership and teams to make informed, evidence-based security decisions.

My goal is to bridge the gap between AI and Security Engineering, developing systems that are adaptive, explainable, and secure by design. I’m driven by curiosity, continuous learning, and a passion for innovation at the intersection of technology and security.
